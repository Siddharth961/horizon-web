Horizon-BVP serves as the debating society of BVCOE, dedicated to fostering debating and world press activities. We function as an experience-sharing platform where seasoned debaters within the club assist others in developing into successful speakers of the future.

Events

Dive into a world of intellectual exploration at HORIZON, where we cultivate skills through Model United Nations, debate competitions, group discussions, interview preparation sessions, AIPPM simulations, journalism workshops, and more. Join us for an enriching experience in thought-provoking discourse and practical learning.
